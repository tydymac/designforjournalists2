Tyler's food picks for NYC

Los Tacos No.1:
This place is good for cheap authentic Mexican tacos which are suprisingly are hard to come by in New York. An added plus,
they recently opened a fish taco place right next door to their original spot.

Emmy Squared Pizza:
If you ever get sick of "New York" style pizza this place is great. It serves "Detroit" style which
is a pan pizza that has crunch cheese along the crust

Ivan Ramen
I am just going to put it out there Ivan Orkin makes the best ramen in NYC. This is also a great spot if you want to
eat alone; the counter is very spacious and when it gets busier you get to skip the wait.

Momofuku Noodle Bar
It pains me to put this on this list. I am not a fan of David Chang so I hate-eat his food. I do not go here for soup, though. The real
winners are the shrimp buns and the spicy lychee slushie. I would go here just for the slushie to be honest.

Waverly Restaurant
This place is my go to "local" spot. While I don't actually consider myself a local, this place will probably not be on anyone
else's "best lists." This is a no fuss dinner in the West Village that is open 24/7. It also probably isn't any better than all
the other diners but I love this place. Either get a cheeseburger deluxe or anything on the breakfast menu.

Molly's Cupcakes
I'll be honest I am not a dessert person and if I do eat dessert, it is pie. So when my roommate bought me
a "peach cobbler cupcake" from this place I was prepared to be dissappointed but, it was awesome. It is weird and
satisfying and really does taste like peach cobbler.

Van Leeuwen Ice Cream
I have never actually been to this ice cream shop but I buy at least 4 pints a month of their
"passion fruit layer cake" ice cream, so it is basically like I have been there.

Burrow
I could understand if you have never been to Burrow. I would have never known about it if I hadn't worked in the same
building. This is a Japanese pastry shop that my coworkers complain that literally everything is good. My favorites are
the matcha madeleine and the foccacia rolls.
